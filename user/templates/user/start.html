Rules

  • There will be no access to the internet during the first round.
  • Each team will be given 10 coding problems based on problem solving.
  • The criteria for points will be the number of problems the team has got correct and the time taken for each.
  • Each Problem consists of 400 points and every 3 mins they will lose 10 points.
  • For the last 30 mins every correct solution will be awarded 100 points.
  • The results and points for each question can be seen in the main page.
  • The team can attempt the problem any number of times
  • The leaderboard with the total points will be displayed on the projector screen.
  • Everyone will be logged out forcefully after 2hrs, and we advise the team members not to logout before 2 hrs is over.
